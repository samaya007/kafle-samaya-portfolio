Billy Beer


For this project I rebranded an old brewing brand called Billy Beer. Where I established the brand identity by crafing a new logo, webpage and product label The main goal was to rebrand is as per the current market trend by giving it a modern look and promote it's rebranding through banner ads and website





Process


The main steps towards rebranding Billy Beer was to keep some old contents and not to change it completely. I wanted people who have already seen the brand to recognize it. I tried my best to reatin key visual elements from the original branding to ensure a sense of continutiy. To make it online presence I created a new webstie from scratch and to place it among the other beverage with modern and creative way, I eventally cretad a new label too with different product mockup.



Logo Design


Here I was assigned to design a makeup brand from scratch, with the main focus on beign natural. The main goal was to promote it as an natural makeup product in the beauty market


Final Logo

Brand Color

Typography

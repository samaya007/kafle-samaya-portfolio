Button Buds


For this project I was responsible for creating a 3D model of an earbuds, it's website, promotioal images and promotioal video.





Process


The process starts out by sketching ideas for the earbuds, doing reseach on internet to find the modern trend.



Earbuds Sketch


While sketching i took used reference from different earbuds avilable in the market and tired to keep it minial as possible so but at the same time make it distinct from other earbuds available in the market.


Earbud Case Sketch


I finalized with this logo for the brand 7. Here i went with nature theme and to showcase the brand as a natural brand I used shades of green with leaf in logo as the main center of attratcion and at the same time the negaive space between top and bottom element also show the number 7.


Typography


Typography is an essential element of any brand, here I have used two kinds of fonts for the brand.

Segoe UI is easy to read and will be used on documents, ads and website header

Secondary font; Satoshi is mostly going to be used on paragraph or h2 element and medium sized will be used.
